1. Overview

Segment is a professional tool designed for efficient and precise video segment extraction.
Key features include:
	•	Multiple segment (In/Out) creation
	•	Batch video exporting
	•	Visual timeline editing
	•	Export to Camera Roll or specific albums
⸻

2. Getting Started

2.1 Importing Videos
	1.	Tap “Import Video” on the home screen.
	2.	Select videos from your Photo Library.
	3.	The app will analyze metadata and generate thumbnails.

⸻

2.2 Adding Segments (In/Out)
	1.	Play the video in the editor.
	2.	Tap “Set In” at the desired starting position.
	3.	Move the playhead and tap “Set Out”.
	4.	Repeat to add multiple segments.
	5.	Segments can be edited or deleted at any time.

⸻

2.3 Exporting Segments

You may:
	•	Export all segments
	•	Export the currently selected segment only
	•	Export into a specific system album
  •	Batch Export
